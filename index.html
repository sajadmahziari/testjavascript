SUMMARY

01. DASHBOARD INTERFACE

The Tesla Model S had the particularity to have a central dashboard inside the car who’s the heart of the system. So we’ve adapted the actual dashboard interface to a sci-fi one in relation with our theme. This includes all the screens from navigating to music… We’ve also added our new feature "Modulation" which allow you to adapt and change the interior of your car.
